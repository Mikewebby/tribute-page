Dr. Michael C Ellis Sr.


Here's a timeline of Pastor Mike

  • 1960 - Born in New Orleans' Lousiana.
  • 1979 - Graduates from the L.B. Landry highschool
  • 1980 - Joins the US Navy
  • 1982 - Marries his wife of 39 years Angela Ellis and together have their first daughter
  • 1983 - Has second daughter
  • 1984 - Selected for Sailor of the quarter(one of the first african americans to win the award).
  • 1986 - Wins sailor of the year.
  • 1988 - Graduates college while still in the navy and being a full time father
  • 1989 Has third daughter
  • 1992 Recieves Master's Degree
  • 2000 - Retires from navy and begins working withing the community of Memphis
  • 2010 - Starts impact Baptist church and ministries
  • 2011 - Starts Impact America Ministries
  • 2012 - Joined Facebook. Follow him?
